# UNIVERSITY ADMISSION PROCESS: STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE

## I. APPLICATION STAGE

### Step 1: Program Selection and Criteria Check
* **Action:** Review the **Admission Criteria** for your desired program (Undergraduate, Graduate, or Doctoral) to ensure you meet all minimum requirements, including GPA, prerequisites, and English proficiency.
* **Key Document:** Ensure your transcripts and test scores are ready.

### Step 2: Online Application Submission
* **Action:** Complete the official **online application form** available on the university's main admissions portal.
* **Fee:** Pay the non-refundable **application fee** (currently $75 USD or equivalent). Waivers may be available for specific financial hardship casesâ€”inquire via the Admissions Office.

### Step 3: Required Document Upload
* **Action:** Upload high-quality digital copies of all mandatory documents:
    * Official Transcripts/Mark Sheets (from all prior institutions).
    * Standardized Test Scores (e.g., TOEFL, IELTS, GRE, GMAT, SAT).
    * Statement of Purpose (SOP) or Personal Essay.
    * Curriculum Vitae (CV) or Resume.
    * A copy of your valid Passport/ID (for international students).

### Step 4: Letters of Recommendation (LOR)
* **Action:** The online system will prompt you to enter the contact details (email addresses) of your recommenders.
* **Process:** The recommenders will receive an automated email link to upload their letters directly and confidentially. *Applicants are responsible for ensuring recommenders submit by the deadline.*

## II. EVALUATION AND REVIEW STAGE

### Step 5: Application Review
* **Action:** The Admissions Office verifies the completeness and authenticity of all submitted documents.
* **Committee Review:** Complete applications are forwarded to the respective program's **Faculty Admissions Committee** for academic assessment.

### Step 6: Interview and/or Portfolio (If Applicable)
* **Action:** Highly qualified candidates for competitive programs (e.g., MBA, Ph.D., Fine Arts) will be invited for a **virtual or in-person interview** or requested to submit a **portfolio** within a specific timeframe.

## III. DECISION AND ENROLLMENT STAGE

### Step 7: Final Decision Notification
* **Action:** Applicants will receive an official notification via the online application portal and email, detailing one of three outcomes:
    * **Offer of Admission (Acceptance):** A formal letter of acceptance is issued.
    * **Waitlist:** Applicant meets criteria but acceptance is pending space availability.
    * **Denial:** The application has not been successful.

### Step 8: Acceptance and Deposit
* **Action:** Accepted applicants must formally **accept the offer** through the portal and pay a non-refundable **enrollment deposit** (which is applied to the first semester's tuition) by the deadline specified in the acceptance letter.

### Step 9: Enrollment and Orientation
* **Action:** Upon acceptance, the admitted student will receive details regarding **course registration**, mandatory **International Student Visas (if applicable)**, and **New Student Orientation**.
