ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS - ADAMAS UNIVERSITY

1. Undergraduate (UG) Programs

General Eligibility: - Must have completed 10+2 from a recognized
board. - Minimum 50% marks (relaxation may apply as per rules). -
English should be a compulsory subject (recommended for some programs).

Program-Specific Eligibility: B.Tech (Engineering Programs) - 10+2 with
Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics (PCM). - Minimum 50% marks in PCM
group. - Entrance exams accepted: JEE Main, WBJEE, AUAT.

BBA / B.Com / BCA / B.Sc - 10+2 in any stream. - Minimum 50% overall
marks. - No entrance exam required for some courses (may vary by year).

2. Postgraduate (PG) Programs

General Eligibility: - Must have a Bachelor's degree in a relevant
discipline. - Minimum 50%-55% marks in graduation.

Program-Specific Eligibility: M.Tech - B.Tech/B.E in relevant
engineering discipline. - GATE score preferred. - AUAT may be required.

MBA - Bachelor's degree in any discipline (minimum 50%). - Entrance
exams accepted: CAT, MAT, CMAT, XAT, AUAT. - Personal Interview (PI) may
be required.

MCA - Bachelor's degree with Mathematics in 10+2 or graduation. - AUAT
or other entrance tests accepted.

3. PhD Programs

Eligibility: - Master's degree in relevant field. - Minimum 55% marks in
post-graduation. - Entrance exam + Interview required: UGC NET / CSIR
NET / GATE or AUAT-PhD.

4. Additional Requirements

-   Valid ID proof and academic documents.
-   Passport-size photographs.
-   Transfer Certificate (TC) for UG admissions.
-   Migration Certificate (if from another board/university).
-   Caste Certificate (if applicable).

5. Selection Process

1.  Application submission (online/offline)
2.  Entrance exam (if applicable)
3.  Personal Interview / Counseling
4.  Document verification
5.  Admission confirmation & fee payment
