1. Computer Science Engineering (B.Tech – 4 Years)

Key Highlights: - Industry-focused curriculum designed with IT
experts. - Advanced labs: AI Lab, Cloud Computing Lab, Networking Lab,
Software Engineering Lab. - Project-based learning in every semester. -
Specializations available: AI & ML, Cybersecurity, Data Science, IoT. -
Mandatory 3–6 month industry internship in final year. - Career paths:
Software Engineer, Data Analyst, AI Engineer, Cloud Architect.

2. Mechanical Engineering (B.Tech – 4 Years)

Key Highlights: - Curriculum based on latest industrial mechanics and
manufacturing systems. - Fully equipped labs: Thermodynamics Lab,
CAD/CAM Lab, Machine Design Lab. - Hands-on workshops with 3D printing &
CNC machining. - Industrial visits to manufacturing plants. - Career
paths: Mechanical Designer, Production Engineer, Automotive Engineer.

3. Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA – 3 Years)

Key Highlights: - Updated curriculum as per NEP guidelines. -
Specializations: Marketing, Finance, HRM, Entrepreneurship. - Industry
case studies and business simulations. - Summer internships in reputed
companies. - Career paths: Business Analyst, HR Executive, Marketing
Associate.

4. Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com – 3 Years)

Key Highlights: - Covers Accounting, Finance, Banking, and Business
Law. - Practical training in GST, Tally, Financial Analytics. -
Internship & project work in corporate sectors. - Career paths:
Accountant, Financial Consultant, Tax Analyst.
